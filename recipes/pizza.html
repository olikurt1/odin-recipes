Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Roll and flaten dough with rolling pin
  2. Spin the dough around to flaten it
  3. Take tomato sauce and evenly spread over entire dough/base
  4. Take cheese and shred it with a grater
  5. Evenly sprinkle cheese over entire base
  6. Take oil and evenly pour over base
  7. Put pizza into oven and wait until cooked
