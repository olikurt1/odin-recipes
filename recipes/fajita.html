Ingredients

  • Different colour bell peppers ~ 4
  • Chicken breast ~ 200g
  • tortilla wraps
  • silver onion ~ 50g
  • guacamole and tomato salsa dip
  1. Cut the bell pepper across the naturally made lines
  2. Cut the chicken breast into long thin strips
  3. Peel the onion and cut it into thin strips
  4. Put a tablespoon of oil into a preheated pan and let it simmer
  5. Place the chicken into the pan for around 5 mins
  6. Follow this with adding the peppers and onions
  7. Let pan fry for around 10 mins constantly turning the food
  8. Once complete warm tortillas in a microwave or toast them
